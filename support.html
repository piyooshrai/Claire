Frequently Asked Questions

What is Claire?

Claire is an AI orchestration platform that coordinates complex, multi-step workflows across your entire operation. Unlike simple chatbots or rigid automation, Claire uses reasoning engines to understand context, handle exceptions, and manage the operational complexity that typically requires human oversight.
