Meet Claire

An AI orchestration platform that brings reasoning-powered automation to complex, multi-step workflows across industries.

What Is Claire?

Claire is not a chatbot. She's not a simple automation tool. Claire is a digital labor platform that orchestrates complex workflows with the context awareness and reasoning capability of a senior team member.

Unlike traditional automation that breaks on edge cases, Claire understands context, handles exceptions, and coordinates across systems the way humans do—but with the consistency, availability, and scalability that only AI can provide.

The Problem We're Solving

Modern businesses run on complex workflows that span multiple systems, channels, and stakeholders. These workflows require constant human coordination: routing messages, scheduling resources, updating records, handling exceptions, and keeping everyone informed.

Traditional automation fails because real-world operations are messy. Rules-based systems can't handle the nuance. Chatbots can't orchestrate across systems. And humans can't scale to handle the volume.

How Claire Works

Claire uses advanced reasoning engines to understand context, maintain state across long-running workflows, and coordinate actions across your entire tech stack. She learns your business logic, adapts to exceptions, and handles the operational complexity that typically requires human oversight.

The result: workflows that run smoothly end-to-end, with Claire managing the coordination, humans focusing on strategic decisions, and customers receiving seamless experiences.

What Makes Claire Different

  • Reasoning Over Scripting: Claire understands "why" behind workflows, not just "what" to do. She adapts to context rather than following rigid scripts.
  • True Orchestration: Claire coordinates across your entire operation—CRM, scheduling, communications, billing, documentation—maintaining context throughout.
  • Exception Handling: Real-world workflows are full of edge cases. Claire recognizes when standard processes don't apply and routes appropriately.
  • Multi-Channel Native: Email, SMS, phone, chat, portal—Claire manages all customer touchpoints with consistent context and personality.
  • Industry Specialized: Deep expertise in Healthcare, Legal, Finance, and Hospitality workflows, with universal capabilities for any industry.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Claire doesn't replace your team; she amplifies them by handling operational complexity while keeping humans in control.

Industries We Serve

Claire has specialized orchestration capabilities across multiple industries:

  • Healthcare: Patient journey orchestration from first contact through post-visit follow-up, including scheduling, insurance, prescription management, and care coordination.
  • Legal: Matter lifecycle management, client intake, document coordination, deadline tracking, and multi-party communication across complex legal workflows.
  • Financial Services: Client onboarding, compliance workflows, transaction coordination, client communications, and relationship management.
  • Hospitality: Guest experience orchestration, reservation management, service coordination, and operational synchronization across properties.
  • Universal Orchestration: Manufacturing, retail, logistics, education, real estate, and professional services benefit from Claire's core coordination capabilities.
